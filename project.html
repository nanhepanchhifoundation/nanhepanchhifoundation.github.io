Nurturing a champion

The project involves 10 children below the age of 10. We work on their reading and numeracy. According to the UNICEF, it has been shown conclusively that children who are unable to develop basic foundations of language and literacy and mathematics by the end of Class 3 are at a serious disadvantage; their academic performance continues to remain poor in later years due to which they are unable to cope with expectations at school. To prevent this we will focus on these students from the beginning and by the end of this year. The motive is to improve the number sense and reading comprehension among children.
